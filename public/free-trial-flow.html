The 14-Day Journey

Release. Align. Become.

Every day is mapped. Every touchpoint is intentional. The user moves through all three pillars with Jim guiding every step. Here is the exact flow, day by day.

Days 1 through 5 · Release
What are you carrying?

Jim asks one question: what are you carrying? The first sessions surface what most apps never touch. Users don't just meditate. They begin to see the patterns keeping them stuck.

Day 1 includes the first hypno-meditation: the moment users experience something no other app offers. Jim AI reflections after each session personalize the journey from the very first day.

Critical window: 80% of users drop off within 30 minutes without structure. The morning anchor strategy combats this: sessions are framed as "your morning session" with time-of-day push reminders, delivering 3x higher retention vs. unstructured access.

Days 6 through 10 · Align
The weight lifts. The centering begins.

The energy shifts from clearing to building. Sessions move from letting go to centering. Jim AI remembers what the user shared in Release and weaves it into Align coaching. No other app does this. The intelligence is cumulative.

Day 5-6 transition is celebrated visually. Jim sends a personal message. An email marks the pillar shift: "Release is complete. Welcome to Align." This creates an emotional peak before the next phase.

Push notification opt-in happens at Day 8, after value has been delivered. Headspace/Phiture research shows this timing yields +157% push opt-in uplift vs. asking at install.

After the Trial

Two paths. Both respectful.

50% of eventual conversions happen after the trial ends. The free tier is not a downgrade. It is the long game.

Path 1: Subscribe
Continue becoming

$19.99/mo or $149.99/yr. Unlock Program Two (new content), unlimited Jim AI, post-trial tracks (Pain, Anxiety, Sleep), and the full library.

No coupon. No urgency. Trust-first pricing mirrors Waking Up's model: highest price in category, highest trust, $12M/yr bootstrapped.

Path 2: Stay Free
28% of the library. Jim's daily wisdom continues.

17 sessions permanently free. Jim AI stays (3 messages/day). Daily wisdom quote + micro-reflection. Soft gate on locked content with tailored copy.

The conversion driver is not frustration. It is desire. A few free sessions cost nothing and keep the door open.
