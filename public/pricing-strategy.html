The One Rule
Activation beats everything.

Activated users convert at 5 to 10x the rate of unactivated users. Everything else (trial length, pricing tiers, credit card policy) is secondary to getting someone to their aha moment.

THE POSSIBILITY ADVANTAGE

The 14-day engine IS the activation event. Release, Align, Become is not a feature tour. It is a structured psychological journey that builds the aha moment into the product itself. The pricing model just needs to not get in its way.

"The industry converts through restriction. We convert through completion."

RECOMMENDATION

14-day reverse trial. Full access to everything (the complete Release, Align, Become engine), then downgrade to a meaningful free tier. Users who don't convert keep access to basic meditations. Nobody feels tricked. Nobody hits a wall.

Why reverse trial wins here
01 Loss aversion is real. After 14 days of Jim's voice, personalized AI coaching, and the full pillar journey, losing that feels like losing progress.
02 Soft landing protects reputation. Users who can't pay land on a free tier, not a locked door. They tell friends "I use The Possibility" not "they cut me off."
03 It matches your philosophy. "We convert through completion, not restriction." A reverse trial is the pricing structure version of that belief.

Free Tier Design
Give away the habit.
Charge for the depth.

Get it wrong and you either give away too much (Evernote: 75M users, 1% conversion) or too little (Headspace: removed all free content, bleeding users).

Free Forever
  • 1 to 2 guided meditations per pillar (curated, not random)
  • Daily quote or micro-reflection from Jim
  • Basic session history
  • Community access
Paid Subscriber
  • Full meditation library
  • All 9 hypno-meditation sessions
  • Jim AI coaching (personalized, remembers you)
  • Intelligence loop (emails, push, recommendations)
  • Full Release, Align, Become engine (re-runnable)
KEY INSIGHT

Free users should still feel Jim's voice daily. That is the habit. But AI coaching, hypno-meditations, and the full engine are what they will miss most after the trial. Those create the upgrade pull.

DO NOT

Gate basic meditation behind the paywall. This is what Headspace did. Users called it "bait and switch" and drove Trustpilot to 1.4 stars. A few free sessions cost you nothing and keep the door open. 50% of eventual conversions happen after the formal trial ends.

Scholarship Access
The Waking Up move.

Waking Up is the only meditation app with meaningful Trustpilot trust. The reason: anyone who cannot afford the subscription gets free access, no questions asked.

STRONGLY RECOMMENDED

Offer hardship access. A small, quiet link: "If cost is a barrier, we want you here anyway." No application. No proof. Near-zero marginal cost for a digital product.

01 Eliminates the #1 attack vector. Nobody can say "they only care about money."
02 Generates word of mouth. Waking Up's scholarship program is discussed more than its features.
03 Aligns with Jim's mission. A life coach who truly believes in this work makes it accessible to everyone.
The Transition
Day 14. Where reputations
are made or destroyed.

Achievement-based triggers convert 258% better than "your trial expires tomorrow" emails. Here is the exact sequence.

Day 12
Gentle acknowledgment
"You're nearing the end of the Become phase. Here's what you've built over the last 12 days." Reflect their journey back. No pricing mention.
Day 13
Transparent preview
"Tomorrow your trial completes. Here's what stays free and what changes." Clear, honest. Show exactly what the free tier includes.
Day 14
The single question
"Do you want to continue becoming?" One button. Annual highlighted. Monthly available. Scholarship link visible. No dark patterns.
Day 15+
Soft landing
Free tier. Jim still shows up daily. They can still meditate. They are still a user. 50% of eventual conversions happen post-trial.
NEVER DO

Countdown timers. "Last chance" language. Auto-charging. Hiding the cancel button. Making annual the only visible option. These are what gave Calm an F rating with the BBB.
