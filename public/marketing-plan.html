Intelligent Scheduling

Every message arrives at the right moment.

Not 9am for everyone. Not batch sends. The system learns when each individual user practices and times every notification to that window.

TLDR

Send time computed from last 30 sessions. Quiet hours 10pm-7am. 3 push / 1 email daily cap. Frequency auto-adjusts based on engagement.

Send Time Optimization

Every user has a preferred_send_hour computed from the mode of their last 30 session timestamps.

Fallback: 8am local time for users with fewer than 5 sessions.

All non-urgent pushes are scheduled for the user's preferred hour. System batches sends by timezone + preferred hour.

Personalization Signals

Pillar affinity: Release, Align, Become, or Balanced

Time preference: Morning, evening, or night

Duration preference: Short or long sessions

Music preference: Music lover or voice-only

Mood trend: If declining, auto-recommend Release content

Campaign A/B Testing

Every campaign supports A/B testing. Users assigned via deterministic hash. Winner declared at statistical significance (p < 0.05) or after 7 days. Minimum 500 users per variant. Winning variant automatically replaces campaign content going forward. Continuous optimization.
