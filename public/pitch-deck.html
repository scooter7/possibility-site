The Framework

Release. Align. Become.

Three phases that represent the complete journey to Possibility. This framework is the intellectual architecture of the entire company. It structures the app, the certification school, the book, and every program Jim creates.

Release
"Fear lies. Most of the time, fear is lying. Unless there's a lion coming at us, then we know to run."
Dissolve fear, limiting patterns, and subconscious blocks. As Jim teaches: as soon as you get comfortable with the unknowing, you lower the inflammation in your energy field, your mind, and then your body.
Freedom
Align
"I am what I want to be, whatever it is. I am calm. I am centered. I am a loving person."
Your identity creates your reality. Align rewrites the inner narrative: step into flow, emotional regulation, and a felt sense of peace with where you are.
Peace
Become
"You take one step and the next step follows. Your feet will never fail you."
Reprogram your identity, manifest clear goals, and take aligned action. As Jim says: if you were programmed with it, you can be programmed with something new. Change the program.
Expansion
"If I was programmed with it, then I can be programmed with other things. Let's change the program."Jim Curtis
